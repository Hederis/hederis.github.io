Pricing Options

Hederis is a web-based book design app that lets you create professional-quality print and ebook layouts at the same time. With Hederis, youâ€™re getting a full-featured, cloud-based book production platform. Read more about all the features of our app here.

Hederis gives you:

  • A cloud-based production tool (login anywhere)
  • Control over page counts and hyphenation (ease in typesetting)
  • In-app text editing (keep all your work in one place)
  • Export to print and digital files (simultaneous distribution)
  • A digital archive (repackage backlist and print on demand)
  • A collaborative workspace (shorten the lines of communication)

PLUS:

  • Unlimited users (pay-per-project, not user)
  • A simple user interface
  • Documentation, training materials, and user-support provided
  • No software, no downloads, and no updating required

There are two different pricing tiers to choose from: Galley/Digital mode, and the full Typeset app. Galley/Digital mode is perfect for folks looking to quickly create an eBook or a review copy of their book; the full Typeset app includes additional tools for bringing your print layout through the complete production cycle.
