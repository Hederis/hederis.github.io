Intelligent, automated publishing

Create beautiful books—quickly—with Hederis.

As a book maker, you're crafting a product that's been perfected over centuries, and technology shouldn't get in your way.

Hederis is a ground-breaking platform that gives you the speed of automation, combined with the quality of traditional book layout. From preparing your manuscript for layout, building a design template, making sure your pages are balanced and lines are breaking in the right places, and fine-tuning all the details that reflect your professional standards, Hederis can help.

Hederis gives you book production and design tools to create high-quality print-ready PDFs and ebook files, ready for distribution, in a fraction of the time it takes with traditional book-production processes.
