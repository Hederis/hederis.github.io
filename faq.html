Support

Where can I learn how to use Hederis? Click here to see our online documentation.
Can I collaborate with other people on my team with Hederis? Yes! It's easy to invite other people in your organization to collaborate on a project in Hederis. That way you can be sure that editors, production, designers, and marketing all have access to the most up-to-date files.

Can I use Hederis to create files for galleys? Absolutely! Hederis is a great solution for getting files for galleys out the door as quickly as possible. Our automated style tagging, built-in design templates, and ability to quickly fix paging issues in the browser make Hederis ideal for producing both print and digital galleys. You can then export a new Word document with your changes to move into production.
Do you offer customer support packages? All users have access to our online training and support materials. Technical

What kinds of files can Hederis export? Hederis can export your book layout as a print-ready PDF, reflowable EPUB, Kindle-optimized EPUB, HTML file, or a Microsoft Word .docx file.
What kind of manuscript files can Hederis import? Hederis can import Microsoft Word .docx files.
Will my computer work with your app? The Hederis app runs right in your web browser--you don't need to install any special software. Our app has been optimized to run in Google Chrome, and will run on any computer running Google Chrome with an internet connection.
Does Hederis support images? Yes! Hederis supports in-line and full-page images, as well as EPUB cover images. Simply tag the image-placement location in the manuscript. You can even resize images and convert them to grayscale in the app.
What kind of file validation do you do? You can run the standard EPUBCheck validation tool right from your project Dashboard. We recommend that you run the epubcheck validation tool on your files before distribution, to ensure that there are no errors. We also recommend running your own preflight validation on PDFs according to your printer's recommendations.
Where can I go if I have other technical questions? Our User Documentation has lots of info about technical aspects of using the app, and you can always ask us directly by emailing sales@hederis.com.
