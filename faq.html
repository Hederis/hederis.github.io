Frequently Asked Questions

Support

Where can I learn how to use Hederis? Click here to see our online documentation.
Do you offer customer support packages? All users have access to our online training and support materials. In addition, all project bundles come with full-service email support for all included projects. If you are interested in custom virtual training or enterprise-support, please contact sales@hederis.com to discuss options and pricing.

Technical

What kinds of files does Hederis export? Hederis can export your book layout as a print-ready PDF, reflowable EPUB, Kindle-optimized EPUB, HTML file, or a Microsoft Word .docx file.

Pricing

How much does Hederis cost? We have plans for single projects, ebook-only projects, and discounts for buying bundles of projects in advance. Please see our Pricing page for more details.
How can I try Hederis without commitment? We offer a free first project for new organizations. Please email sales@hederis.com for more information.
